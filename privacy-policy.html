Privacy Policy

How Prestige Electric collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Prestige Electric ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us about electrical services.

Information we collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Project details submitted through quote, opt-in, or contact forms
  • Communications you send to us by phone, email, or text message

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and general usage data through standard website analytics tools.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to service requests, estimates, and customer inquiries
  • Schedule electrical service and provide project updates
  • Send service-related communications you opt in to receive
  • Improve our website, marketing, and customer experience
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud or misuse of our services

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, such as website hosting, email, or customer communication tools, only as needed to provide services to you.

Text and email communications

If you opt in to receive emails or text messages from Prestige Electric, you consent to receive service updates and related communications. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of marketing messages at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the message or by contacting us directly.

Cookies and tracking

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality and understand how visitors use the site. You can adjust cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries, provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we maintain about you, subject to applicable law. Contact us using the information below to make a request.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
