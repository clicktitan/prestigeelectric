Safety first

When should you call an electrician?

Call a professional if you notice breakers tripping often, hot outlets, buzzing switches, dimming lights, burning smells, exposed wiring, or power problems after adding new equipment.

What are common signs I should call an electrician?

Frequent breaker trips, flickering lights, warm outlets, buzzing panels, burning smells, outdated wiring, or power loss are all signs that a qualified electrician should inspect the system.
